About project

We create events that change perception

Silent Wave is a community of people in love with sound, movement, and the "here and now" moment. We live in Burgas and know how to turn any spot in the city into a stage.

🎧

Hi-Fi headphones — 3 music waves

World hits, rock, electronics — simultaneously on one dance floor. Everyone chooses their own.

📍

Any location without noise restrictions

Beach, park, roof, yard — wherever the atmosphere is born.

🌊

Born by the sea, in Burgas

Sea garden, Old town, north beach — all this is our stage.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before your first Silent Wave in Burgas.

🎧

Still have a question?

Message us — we will reply within an hour.

Message on WhatsApp
Will the headphones fall off during active dancing?▼
Designed specifically for active movements. They fit snugly, weigh about 200g. Hundreds of guests at Ecstatic Dance — not a single incident.
How long does the battery last?▼
Up to 10-12 hours of continuous playback. From evening sunset to dawn.
Do I need internet to work?▼
No! The system works on its own UHF radio channel. Beach, forest, roof — anywhere without Wi-Fi.
What is the range?▼
Up to 100-150 meters. Guests freely move around the area without losing signal.
Do I need experience for Ecstatic Dance?▼
No experience is needed. Ecstatic Dance is freedom of movement without rules. The body knows what to do itself.
Can an event be held outside Burgas?▼
Yes! We work all over Bulgaria: Sozopol, Varna, Nessebar, Plovdiv, Sofia. Contact us and we will calculate the trip.
